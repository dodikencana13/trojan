# 💰 Trojan Chain Tokenomics (T-ETH)

The Trojan Chain ecosystem is powered by **T-ETH**, the native utility token designed to secure the network and incentivize the Relayer network.

## 📊 Token Distribution
**Total Supply**: 1,000,000,000 T-ETH (Fixed)

| Allocation | Percentage | Amount (T-ETH) | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Genesis Fund** | 40% | 400,000,000 | Core development and initial liquidity. |
| **Relayer Incentives** | 30% | 300,000,000 | Rewards for nodes paying user gas fees. |
| **Public Ecosystem** | 20% | 200,000,000 | Community grants and airdrops. |
| **Treasury** | 10% | 100,000,000 | Long-term stability and governance. |

## ⚙️ Token Utility
T-ETH serves three primary purposes within the Trojan ecosystem:

1. **Relayer Collateral**: Relayers must stake T-ETH to be eligible to process gasless transactions. This ensures they act honestly.
2. **Governance**: T-ETH holders can vote on network upgrades, fee structures, and treasury allocations.
3. **Network Security**: T-ETH is used in the underlying consensus mechanism to secure the Trojan Chain.

## 📉 Deflationary Mechanism
To ensure long-term value, Trojan Chain implements a **Burn-on-Relay** mechanism. A small percentage of the fees paid by corporate sponsors to Relayers is permanently burned, reducing the total supply of T-ETH over time.
