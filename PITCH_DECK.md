# 📈 Trojan Chain Investor Pitch Deck (Executive Summary)

## 1. The Opportunity
The "Gas Wall" is the single biggest barrier to the mass adoption of blockchain. Users are deterred by the need to buy native tokens just to send a transaction. 

**Trojan Chain** solves this by creating a "Sponsorship Layer," making the blockchain experience invisible and free for the end-user.

## 2. The Product: Trojan Chain
A high-performance L1 blockchain featuring:
- **Gasless Execution**: Integrated EIP-712 signature relaying.
- **Production-Ready RPC**: High-throughput JSON-RPC interface.
- **Persistent State**: Optimized SQLite/MongoDB storage for rapid state transitions.
- **EVM Compatibility**: Low friction for existing Ethereum developers.

## 3. The Market Gap
Current "gasless" solutions are often centralized wrappers or complex L2 setups. Trojan Chain integrates the Relayer directly into the L1 architecture, providing a native, streamlined experience.

## 4. Monetization Strategy
Trojan Chain operates on a **B2B Sponsorship Model**:
- **Corporate Paymasters**: Companies pay the Trojan Network to sponsor their users' gas (e.g., a game company pays so their players don't have to).
- **Relayer Fees**: Relayers earn a small spread on the sponsorship fees.
- **T-ETH Appreciation**: As the ecosystem grows, the demand for T-ETH for staking and governance increases.

## 5. Roadmap
- **Phase 1 (Current)**: Core engine and gasless relay logic completed.
- **Phase 2**: Deployment of the Public Mainnet & Decentralized Validator Set.
- **Phase 3**: Launch of the Trojan SDK for one-click DApp integration.
- **Phase 4**: Cross-chain bridging with Ethereum and Arbitrum.

## 6. The Ask
We are seeking strategic partnerships and seed funding to move from the current functional prototype to a globally distributed, decentralized L1 network.
